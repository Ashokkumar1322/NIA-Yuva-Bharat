1. PREAMBLE & PLANS

This is Your "YUVA BHARAT HEALTH POLICY", which has been issued by Us, relying on the information disclosed by You in Your Proposal for this Policy or its preceding Policy/Policies of which this is a renewal. The terms and conditions set out in this Policy and its Schedule will be the basis for any claim and/or benefit under this Policy. If during the Period of Insurance, You or any Insured Person incurs Hospitalisation Expenses which are Reasonable and Customary, and Medically Necessary for treatment of any Illness or Injury, we will reimburse such expense through the Third-Party Administrator incurred by You in the manner stated herein.

  • Base Plan (Clauses 3.1 to 3.17): Covers Hospitalisation Expenses, Organ Transplant, Hospital Cash, Road Ambulance, Congenital Diseases, Cataract, Hazardous Sports, Shared Accommodation, Modern Treatments, Health Check-Up, Medical Second Opinion, Reinstatement of Sum Insured, AYUSH, New Born Baby Cover.
  • Gold Plan (Clauses 3.1 to 3.25): Base plan coverages + Critical Care Benefit, Personal Accident Benefit, Auto TOP-UP, Reinstatement of Sum Insured twice, and Guaranteed Cumulative Bonus.
  • Platinum Plan (Clauses 3.1 to 3.30): Gold plan coverages + Maternity Coverage, Infertility Treatment, Well Baby Cover, and Birth Right Benefit.

2. STANDARD & SPECIFIC DEFINITIONS

ACCIDENT: A sudden, unforeseen and involuntary event caused by external, visible and violent means.

ANY ONE ILLNESS: Continuous Period of Illness and it includes relapse within 45 days from the date of last consultation with the Hospital where treatment may have been taken.

AYUSH HOSPITAL: A Healthcare facility registered with the local authorities, having at least 5 in-patient beds, maintaining daily records and under the continuous supervision of a qualified registered AYUSH Medical Practitioner round the clock.

CASHLESS FACILITY: A facility extended by Us to You where the payments, of the costs of treatment undergone by You, are directly made to the Network provider to the extent of pre-authorization approved.

CONGENITAL ANOMALY: Refers to a condition(s) present since birth, which is abnormal with reference to form, structure or position. Internal anomalies are hidden; external are visible.

PRE-EXISTING CONDITION/DISEASE: Any condition, ailment, Injury or Illness diagnosed or for which medical advice or treatment was recommended/received from a physician not more than 24 months prior to the date of commencement of policy issued by the insurer.

CRITICAL ILLNESSES (Clause 2.9): Covers Cancer of Specified Severity (excluding Carcinoma in situ, early prostate cancers, tumors in presence of HIV); Myocardial Infarction (First Heart Attack); Open Chest CABG; Open Heart Replacement/Repair of Heart Valves; Coma of Specified Severity (lasting at least 96 hours on life support); Kidney Failure requiring regular dialysis/transplant; Stroke resulting in permanent symptoms; Major Organ/Bone Marrow Transplant; Permanent Paralysis of Limbs; Motor Neurone Disease; Multiple Sclerosis; Third Degree Burns (covering >20% body surface); Major Head Trauma; End-Stage Lung Failure; End-Stage Liver Failure.

INFERTILITY: The failure to achieve a clinical pregnancy after 12 months or more of regular unprotected sexual intercourse.

Room Rent Limits: Single AC Room for Sum Insured bands of 5L/10L/15L. Deluxe AC Room for Sum Insured bands of 25L/50L/75L/1Cr. Proportionate Deduction applies to Associate Medical Expenses if a higher room is opted.

Organ Transplant: Reimburses expenses incurred on the donor (excluding organ cost, pre and post hospitalisation) subject to the available recipient's Sum Insured.

Hospital Cash Benefit: Paid up to a maximum of 5 days for Any One Illness (exceeding 24 hours): 5L/10L/15L (Rs. 500/day), 25L (Rs. 750/day), 50L (Rs. 1,000/day), 75L (Rs. 1,500/day), 1Cr (Rs. 2,000/day).

Road Ambulance Limits: Max per illness: 5L/10L/15L (Rs. 5,000), 25L (Rs. 7,500), 50L (Rs. 10,000), 75L (Rs. 15,000), 1Cr (Rs. 20,000).

Shared Accommodation Cash Benefit: Opting for shared accommodation provides additional daily cash benefits from Rs. 750 to Rs. 1,750 per day depending on Sum Insured band.

Cataract Caps: Maximum per eye per policy period: 5L/10L/15L (Rs. 50,000), 25L (Rs. 75,000), 50L (Rs. 1,00,000), 75L (Rs. 1,25,000), 1Cr (Rs. 1,50,000).

4. EXCLUSIONS & WAITING PERIODS

Pre-Existing Diseases (PED): Excluded until 24 months of continuous coverage expire.

First 30 Days Waiting Period: Illnesses excluded inside first 30 days of initial risk start date, except accidents.

90 Days Specific Waiting Period: Diabetes Mellitus, Hypertension, and Cardiac Conditions.

12 Months Specific Waiting Period: Benign tumors/cysts, Cataract, ENT disorders, Hernia, Hydrocele, Piles/Fissures/Fistula, Sinusitis, Spinal Diseases (unless accident), Stone in Gall Bladder/Bile duct, Urinary system stones, Renal failure, and Internal Congenital anomalies.

24 Months Specific Waiting Period: External Congenital anomalies, Joint Replacements due to degenerative conditions, Osteoarthritis/Osteoporosis, Mental Illness, Age-Related Macular Degeneration (ARMD), Genetic disorders, Infertility treatments, and Maternity Coverage.

Permanent Exclusions: Obesity or weight control surgeries (unless meeting specific complex therapeutic medical benchmarks like BMI >=40); Change of Gender treatments; Cosmetic/Plastic surgery (unless post-accident/burn/cancer reconstruction); Breach of Law; Excluded explicit clinical providers; Alcohol/Substance abuse treatment; Dietary supplements without standard script; Refractive error corrections under 7.5 diopters; Unproven/Experimental procedures; Domiciliary hospitalisation; War and nuclear radiation.

5. GENERAL TERMS & CONDITIONS

Cancellation: Policyholder can cancel at any point with 7 days written notice. Premium refunds are applicable lineally on a short scale format if no claims have occurred. Insurers can terminate with 15 days notice exclusively on proven grounds of fraud or non-disclosure.

Free Look Period: 30 days validation span from physical policy acquisition to request full cancellation and premium refund if unsatisfied with terms.

Claim Settlement and Window: Company must settle or reject claims within 15 days of receiving the final necessary verification documents. Delays incur penal interest at 2% above the prevailing financial year RBI bank rate.

Zone Classification: Premium computed based on Zone 1 (Delhi NCR, Mumbai/Suburban, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Surat, Ahmedabad, Vadodara) or Zone 2 (Rest of India). If a Zone 2 policyholder takes non-accidental planned treatment in Zone 1, a strict 10% co-payment applies to the admissible claim amount.

Long Term Policies: Sum Insured, waiting periods, sub-limits, and bonuses act independently per year and cannot be carried over, advanced, or stacked cumulatively.
